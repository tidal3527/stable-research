# Welcome

*Stable Research’s Knowledge Base offers deeply researched, succinct profiles of blockchains, wallets, protocols and related DeFi infrastructure, as well as original research exploring key technical and economic developments in the ecosystem.*

Each post is designed to give you a concrete, well-rounded understanding of the subject, whether it's a protocol’s mechanism, a chain’s architecture or a wallet’s custody model. Our goal is clarity and insight.
